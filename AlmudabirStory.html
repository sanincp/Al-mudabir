Starting Small, Growing Strong

The Emergence of a Brand!Upon relocating to Saudi Arabia, my focus sharpened on personal growth and building something significant from my experiences. The memories of home tugged at my heart, igniting a passion to support my homeland in any way I could—through creating job opportunities or providing financial assistance. I began my entrepreneurial journey by opening a small supermarket in Saudi. This venture allowed me to grasp the essentials of business management and experiment with various strategies for success. Not every attempt yielded positive outcomes, but each setback offered valuable lessons. It made me ponder a bigger vision: Why not expand my efforts on a global scale During this period, I encountered Sayed Ahmed Jifri, a man whose wisdom and unwavering faith left a lasting impression on me. His insights during our discussions emphasized the vital role of creating opportunities and employing others. His words resonated deeply within me and galvanized my ambition to become an entrepreneur. Though he has since passed, his prayers and guidance remain a vital source of inspiration in my life. With the blessing and encouragement from the late Syed Ahamed Jifri, we launched Al Mudabir and quickly began to broaden our horizons. We introduced the AlMudabir portal to embrace the IT sector, followed by a foray into real estate with Adana Real Estate. This trajectory of success has only continued to gain momentum. Today, Al Mudabir is on a mission to expand further and establish a lasting legacy across various regions.

Our Entities

Over the past 21 years, Al Mudabir Group has grown into a wide network of successful businesses, offering creative solutions to meet changing market needs. Our ongoing growth shows our dedication to adding value, building strong partnerships, and creating opportunities. As we look ahead, we are focused on delivering high-quality results and providing unmatched benefits to our clients and partners.

The Milestones We've Reached

As explained earlier, for over 21 years, Al Mudabir Group has proudly served the food industry, meeting one of the world’s most essential needs. Since opening our first supermarket in Riyadh in 2003, we've built a strong foundation in this vital and ever-growing sector.

Today, we operate three supermarkets under the Al Mudabir brand, but by 2030, we aim to expand to 300 locations across Saudi Arabia and the UAE. With new corporate offices in Dubai and Riyadh, we’re setting the stage for growth.

Our next step? Transforming each store to meet international standards, ensuring quality and consistency.

Starting Small, Growing Strong

The Rise of a Brand! When I moved to Saudi, my mind was fully focused on growth and making something meaningful out of my journey. Memories of home filled me with emotion, and I felt a strong urge to support my homeland however I could—whether by creating opportunities or sending financial help. I started small, opening a supermarket in Saudi. To my surprise, it quickly became successful. This made me think, Why not take it a step further? Why not try it on a global level? Meanwhile, I happened to meet Sayed Ahmed Jifri, a man of wisdom and deep faith. During our conversation, he shared not only his prayers but also the importance of creating opportunities and giving jobs to others. His words deeply inspired me and sparked my journey to become an entrepreneur. Though he is no longer with us, his prayers and advice continue to have a profound impact on my life, guiding me even today. With the support, well-wishes, and prayers of Late Syed Ahamed Jifri, we established Al Mudabir and soon expanded our reach into new fields. We ventured into IT through the AlMudabbir portal and later into real estate with Adana Real Estate. The success story continued to grow from there. Today,Al Mudabbir is on a mission—branching out further and building a legacy across different regions.

Our Vision

Our Entities

Over the past 21 years, Al Mudabbir Group has grown into a wide network of successful businesses, offering creative solutions to meet changing market needs. Our ongoing growth shows our dedication to adding value, building strong partnerships, and creating opportunities. As we look ahead, we are focused on delivering high-quality results and providing unmatched benefits to our clients and partners.

The Milestones We've Reached

As explained earlier, for over 21 years, Al Mudabir Group has proudly served the food industry, meeting one of the world’s most essential needs. Since opening our first supermarket in Riyadh in 2003, we've built a strong foundation in this vital and ever-growing sector.

Today, we operate three supermarkets under the Al Mudabir brand, but by 2030, we aim to expand to 300 locations across Saudi Arabia and the UAE. With new corporate offices in Dubai and Riyadh, we’re setting the stage for growth.

Our next step? Transforming each store to meet international standards, ensuring quality and consistency.

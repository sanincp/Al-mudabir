Nihal Danish Muhammad
Nihal Danish Muhammad is the Managing Director of Al Mudabir Group of Companies, a well-respected business leader with years of experience in successfully managing and growing businesses across various industries. Under his leadership, Al Mudabir has expanded its operations, achieved consistent growth, and earned a reputation for excellence and reliability. As Managing Director, Nihal Danish Muhammad is known for his clear vision, strategic thinking, and strong leadership skills. He believes in teamwork, innovation, and creating long-term value for clients, partners, and stakeholders. His focus on customer satisfaction, quality services, and ethical business practices has helped the company stand out in competitive markets.
Sameer Ali Meppoyil

Known for his leadership in sustainability initiatives, he has played a pivotal role in transforming companies. As the CEO of Al Mudabbir, Sameer’s experience has been instrumental in driving growth, innovation, and sustainable success. With a strong focus on delivering results and fostering collaboration, Sameer continues to lead Al Mudabbir towards new heights.
Saud bin Khaled Al-Subaie

Under his leadership, the company has grown exponentially, achieving new milestones. Saud is known for his strategic thinking, dedication, and ability to make effective decisions. His vision and commitment to excellence have made him a driving force behind the company's achievements. With his focus on growth and innovation, Saud bin Khaled Al-Subaie continues to lead Al Mudabir Group to new heights.
Muhammad Mepoyil
As the Founder and Chairman of Al Mudabir Group, Mr. Muhammed Meppoyil brings dynamic leadership and over 30 years of diverse experience to the forefront. With a remarkable background encompassing operations, finance, and entrepreneurship, he has played pivotal roles in various corporations, including his current position as Chairman of Almudabir Group of Companies. Additionally, he holds significant roles on boards, such as KIMS Hospital in Kerala. Mr. Meppoyil's expertise is a cornerstone for Al Mudabir's success. His proven track record in corporate affairs, private sector operations, and financial management provides invaluable strategic direction. His multifaceted skills extend to leading large groups, fostering competitiveness in challenging markets, and navigating enterprise risks effectively. As the driving force behind Al Mudabir Group, Mr. Meppoyil's active and all-encompassing approach reflects a profound commitment to sector growth. His seasoned leadership ensures Adana benefits from a visionary leader capable of steering the company towards continued success.
John Probandt
John Probandt,a highly respected leader in the financial world. As the youngest vice president in the history of Morgan Stanley (Dean Witter), he set the foundation for a career marked by innovation, leadership, and strategic thinking. His 20+ years in Beijing, working with CITIC Group—the largest financial institution in China—gave him a deep understanding of global markets, partnerships, and navigating complex industries. At Al Mudabir, Mr. John’s expertise plays a critical role in guiding investment strategies, identifying high-potential ventures, and supporting long-term growth. His experience with billion-dollar companies and navigating global financial systems helps Al Mudabir connect with the right partners and achieve its vision of creating value-driven businesses.
Muhammed Ramees
Muhammed Ramees is the Managing Director of Al Mudabir Group of Companies. With exceptional skills in accounting and financial management, he plays a key role in leading the company to success. His expertise in handling accounts ensures the smooth operation of the business and helps in making strong financial decisions. Under his leadership, Al Mudabir Group has grown steadily, reflecting his dedication, hard work, and strategic vision. H.H Sheikh Khalid bin Muhamed bin Salem Al Qasimi
H.H Sheik Khaled Bin Mohamed Bin Salem Al Qasimi brings a wealth of expertise from a diverse background encompassing military, law enforcement, and academic achievements. His journey began with an exemplary military career in the United Kingdom, where he achieved the rank of Lieutenant. Subsequently, he joined the UAE Armed Forces Special Operations Group and concluded his military tenure with the Emiri Guard in Abu Dhabi. After a transformative decade rising to Major at the Ras Al Khaimah Police General Command, Mr. Al Qasimi's honed skills ensure exceptional leadership in high-pressure situations. With extensive training across various disciplines, both domestically and internationally, H.H. Al Qasimi's expertise significantly benefits Adana. As Patron, his leadership ensures ALMUDABIR GROUP leverages his wealth of knowledge to enhance security measures and strategic planning reinforces the company's commitment to excellence and safety in OUR GROUP

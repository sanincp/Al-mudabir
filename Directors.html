Nihal Danish Muhammad
Nihal Danish Muhammad is the Managing Director of Al Mudabbir Group of Companies, a well-respected business leader with years of experience in successfully managing and growing businesses across various industries. Under his leadership, Al Mudabbir has expanded its operations, achieved consistent growth, and earned a reputation for excellence and reliability. As Managing Director, Nihal Danish Muhammad is known for his clear vision, strategic thinking, and strong leadership skills. He believes in teamwork, innovation, and creating long-term value for clients, partners, and stakeholders. His focus on customer satisfaction, quality services, and ethical business practices has helped the company stand out in competitive markets.
Sameer Ali Meppoyil
Sameer Ali Meppoyil is a seasoned professional with expertise in financial analysis, budget management, risk management, strategic planning, and market analysis. Known for his leadership in sustainability initiatives, he has played a pivotal role in transforming companies. As the CEO of Al Mudabbir, Sameer’s experience has been instrumental in driving growth, innovation, and sustainable success. His ability to navigate complex challenges and develop effective strategies has greatly contributed to the company’s success. With a strong focus on delivering results and fostering collaboration, Sameer continues to lead Al Mudabbir towards new heights.
John Probandt
John Probandt,a highly respected leader in the financial world. As the youngest vice president in the history of Morgan Stanley (Dean Witter), he set the foundation for a career marked by innovation, leadership, and strategic thinking. His 20+ years in Beijing, working with CITIC Group—the largest financial institution in China—gave him a deep understanding of global markets, partnerships, and navigating complex industries. At Al Mudabir, Mr. John’s expertise plays a critical role in guiding investment strategies, identifying high-potential ventures, and supporting long-term growth. His experience with billion-dollar companies and navigating global financial systems helps Al Mudabir connect with the right partners and achieve its vision of creating value-driven businesses.
Muhammad Ramees
Muhammed Ramees is the Managing Director of Al Mudabbir Group of Companies. With exceptional skills in accounting and financial management, he plays a key role in leading the company to success. His expertise in handling accounts ensures the smooth operation of the business and helps in making strong financial decisions. Under his leadership, Al Mudabbir Group has grown steadily, reflecting his dedication, hard work, and strategic vision. Known for his professionalism and commitment, Muhammed Ramees is a driving force behind the company’s achievements.
H.H Sheik Khaled Bin Mohamed Bin Salem Al Qasimi
H.H Sheik Khaled Bin Mohamed Bin Salem Al Qasimi brings a wealth of expertise from a diverse background encompassing military, law enforcement, and academic achievements. His journey began with an exemplary military career in the United Kingdom, where he achieved the rank of Lieutenant. Subsequently, he joined the UAE Armed Forces Special Operations Group and concluded his military tenure with the Emiri Guard in Abu Dhabi. After a transformative decade rising to Major at the Ras Al Khaimah Police General Command, Mr. Al Qasimi's honed skills ensure exceptional leadership in high-pressure situations. With extensive training across various disciplines, both domestically and internationally, H.H. Al Qasimi's expertise significantly benefits Adana. As Patron, his leadership ensures Al Mudabir GROUP leverages his wealth of knowledge to enhance security measures and strategic planning reinforces the company's commitment to excellence and safety in OUR GROUP

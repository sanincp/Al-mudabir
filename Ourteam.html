Meet the Faces Driving Al Mudabir Forward

Our dedicated team of professionals works together to bring innovation, efficiency, and success to every project we undertake. With a shared commitment to excellence, we strive to exceed expectations and achieve outstanding results.

Ashutosh Kumar Singh

Business Development Manager & Sales Manager

Ashutosh Kumar is the Director of Sales and Business Development Manager at Almudabbir Group, where he brings a wealth of experience in the GCC Market. With a passion for innovation and growth, Ashutosh excels in conducting insightful market research and leading dynamic teams to achieve ambitious company goals. His expertise spans various sectors, including real estate, home automation, and IT solutions, allowing him to identify unique business opportunities that drive success. Known for his strategic mindset and strong communication skills, Ashutosh is dedicated to delivering exceptional value to clients while ensuring Almudabbir Group remains a leader in the industry

Jiwanti Bisht

Senior Portfolio Manager

Jiwanti Bisht is a Senior Portfolio Manager at Almudabbir Group, where she plays a crucial role in managing diverse portfolios that encompass IT solutions, real estate, and food products across the GCC region. With over three years of experience, Jiwanti excels in analyzing market trends and identifying growth opportunities. Her strong communication skills and client-focused approach enable her to build lasting relationships while leading teams to achieve their objectives. Committed to delivering innovative solutions, Jiwanti is dedicated to delivering innovative solutions that meet the needs of clients and contribute to Al Mudabbir Group's success.

Syed Nabeel Ali

Senior Portfolio Manager

Syed Nabeel Ali is a dynamic Sales Executive with over 5 years of experience in the real estate and business development sectors across the GCC. Currently serving as a Senior Portfolio Manager at Almudabbir Group, he brings a proven track record of driving business growth and building strong client relationships. Nabeel's expertise spans property management, sales strategies, and market expansion, with a demonstrated ability to identify new opportunities and deliver exceptional value. His strategic approach, combined with strong communication skills, makes him a key contributor to Almudabbir Group's success in the competitive market.

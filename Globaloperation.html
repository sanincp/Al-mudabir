Our journey began with a small supermarket dedicated to providing the community with essential everyday items. As we evolved, we ventured into new sectors such as IT and real estate, transforming into a diverse business group. Our team is composed of individuals from various nationalities, fostering a vibrant and inclusive work environment. We take pride in offering meaningful opportunities that enhance the lives of our employees and their families.

The Dawn of Al Mudabir

I began my entrepreneurial journey by opening a small supermarket in Saudi. This venture allowed me to grasp the essentials of business management and experiment with various strategies for success. Not every attempt yielded positive outcomes, but each setback offered valuable lessons. It made me ponder a bigger vision: Why not expand my efforts on a global scale During this period, I encountered Sayed Ahmed Jifri, a man whose wisdom and unwavering faith left a lasting impression on me. His insights during our discussions emphasized the vital role of creating opportunities and employing others. His words resonated deeply within me and galvanized my ambition to become an entrepreneur. Though he has since passed, his prayers and guidance remain a vital source of inspiration in my life.

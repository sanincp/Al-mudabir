The Dawn of Al Mudabbir

Opening a supermarket in Saudi, it was the beginning of it all. To my surprise, it quickly became successful. This made me think, Why not take it a step further? Why not try it on a global level?

Meanwhile, I happened to meet Sayed Ahmed Jifri, a man of wisdom and deep faith. During our conversation, he shared not only his prayers but also the importance of creating opportunities and giving jobs to others. His words deeply inspired me and sparked my journey to become an entrepreneur. Though he is no longer with us, his prayers and advice continue to have a profound impact on my life, guiding me even today.
